Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy describes how Holy Joe Coffee collects, uses, and safeguards your data.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when voluntarily provided, such as through:

  • Booking request forms on our website
  • Direct email or phone communication

This may include your name, email address, phone number, event details, and organization name.

How We Use Your Information

Information is used solely to respond to inquiries, coordinate coffee cart services, and communicate about your event. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you provide. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cancellations made within 48 hours of the scheduled event may incur fees. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule or cancel.
