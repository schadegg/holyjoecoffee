Our Story

How It Started

Born from a Desire to Serve

Holy Joe Coffee was born from a desire to create a warm, welcoming space wherever people gather — from parish festivals and school events to weddings and workplace celebrations.

Our Patron

Inspired by St. Joseph

Inspired by the quiet strength and faithful care of St. Joseph, we see each event as an opportunity to serve with humility, attentiveness, and joy. Every drink is prepared with care, but our deeper hope is that people feel seen, welcomed, and at home.

What We Serve

Proudly Serving Catholic Coffee

We proudly serve Catholic Coffee, with roasts named after beloved saints and devotions. It's a simple way to weave prayerful intention into the rhythm of daily life.

Our mission is to fuel both body and spirit — bringing people together, fostering meaningful connection, and honoring the dignity of daily work through the simple gift of coffee.
